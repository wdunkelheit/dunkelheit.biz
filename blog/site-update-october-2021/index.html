The articles:

Website Update

Did you notice the Halloween Apus? It’s Spooktober! 👻 I experimented with a few designs for them but this one seems the least intrusive.

I also finally decided on the background colour for the site. I settled for ThinkPad grey, also known as HTML notation #222222.

I have been meaning to try out OpenBSD for a long time, I plan to install it on hardware in the near future and report my thoughts on the experience here.

Returning to University

I am returning to University, it may or may not have an effect on how often I can post content to the site.
